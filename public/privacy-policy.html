Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 6, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to The Subgames ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our web application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google or Apple, we collect your email address, display name, and profile picture.
  • Creator Profiles: If you register as a creator, we collect your display name, content URL (YouTube, Twitch, etc.), and profile information.
  • Social Media Links: URLs to your social media profiles (optional).

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Game Data: Game scores, reaction times, and play history.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, and interaction patterns.
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Track game scores and points for creators
  • Display creator profiles on leaderboards
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Communicate with you about service updates

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Firebase/Google Cloud: Our hosting and database provider
  • Authentication Providers: Google and Apple for sign-in services
  • Social Media APIs: YouTube, Twitch for fetching creator profile data

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure cloud storage with Firebase
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection (guest mode)
  • Export your data

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Game history and leaderboard data is retained for daily cycles. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time.

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such data, we will delete it promptly.

9. Cookies and Tracking

We use browser local storage to maintain your session. We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
